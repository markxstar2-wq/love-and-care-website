Our Story

The Beginning

FOUNDED 1998

Love and Care Gift Ministries was born out of a passion to see communities transformed by the gospel and practical acts of kindness.

Our Vision

To be a global beacon of Christ's love, where faith leads to tangible transformation in every heart and community we touch.

Our Mission

To demonstrate God's grace by providing spiritual nourishment, quality education, and compassionate care to those in need.
