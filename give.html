Building His House

"Unless the Lord builds the house, its builders labor in vain."

Current Phase: Structural Walling

Building a Sanctuary of Hope

Our church construction project is more than just bricks and mortar; it is the creation of a permanent home for the Gospel and a refuge for the community of Kawanda-Kirinyabigo.

Modern Sanctuary

A 500-seat capacity hall designed for worship and community gatherings.

Children's Center

Dedicated classrooms for Sunday school and early childhood education.

Community Hub

Spaces for youth empowerment, counseling, and vocational training.

Safety & Security

A fully walled perimeter and secure entrance for all congregants.

What Your Gift Buys

Your contributions currently go toward purchasing **cement, steel bars, and bricks** to complete the main sanctuary walls. No gift is too small to make a difference.
